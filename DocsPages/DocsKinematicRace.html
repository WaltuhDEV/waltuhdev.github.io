SCRIPT VARS

To  start, Put the char scene into your testing scene or map and select it

Next, You will find a script vars on the char details , This is what we need

Speed -- The speed of the player
Rotation speed -- The speed of rotation in tps mode
Speed increase -- How much will be added to player when
 the up key is hold
max speed -- the limit of speed the player can reach
gravity -- explains it self (How much will be added the player
when the player is in air so make sure to put - )
drift -- enable or disable drift
drift factor -- explains it self
camera distance -- how much will the camera will be away
from player
camera height -- how high will be the camera
zoom factor -- how much will be added when trying to zoom
min zoom -- Start of zoom
max zoom -- limit of zoom
camera mode -- set the camera mode (1 is tps 2 is fps 3 is cuztps)
Mouse sensitivity -- sensitivity or speed of the mouse
disable rotation -- disable or enable rotation
debug -- enable or disable debug
enable respawn -- enable or disable respawn
respawn pos -- where will the player get respawned

death threshold -- when player goes to that y pos it will get
respawned

Camera Settings

Press H to Increase the height 
Press J to decrease it
Press N to increase the dis
Press M to decrease it

ALL of these are changeable in input map!
